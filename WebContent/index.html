Programs offered :

  • Beginning Spanish : This helps to start learning new vocabulary in Spanish, Pronunciation, basics of Spanish grammar

  • Advanced Spanish : In this course, you'll find 100% natural, flowing Spanish conversation between a range of different Spanish speakers, and a range of different accents. You'll learn local lingo from Colombia, Cuba, Mexico, and Spain, as well as get an ear for their accents

  • Conversational Spanish : In order to learn to speak Spanish conversationally, you need lots of one-on-one practice with a native speaker. With our course, you will get the practice you need to allow you to reach a level where you can finally have real conversations with native speakers.

  • Advanced Conversational Spanish : If you would like to take a more well rounded approach to learning Spanish then enroll to this course

  • Spanish Grammar and Compostion : To review and strengthen your understanding and use of major concepts of Spanish grammar including grammatical tenses, the subjunctive, pronominal verbs, passive forms, prepositions, and subordination, among other grammatical points.

  • Cultural Treasures of Mexico : Home to more than 44,000 archeological sites, Mexico ranks third in World Heritage Cities. Mexico is the hotspot for world-class culture, seamlessly bridging together both the past and the future. Enroll in this to explore more about Mexico
